Driveway Planning Sequence

  1. Measure driveway size, extension area, apron, and access before pricing.
  2. Separate removal, base prep, reinforcement, thickness, finish, and drainage costs.
  3. Check apron, curb, and local permit requirements before scheduling.
  4. Compare concrete, asphalt, and paver options with the same dimensions.
